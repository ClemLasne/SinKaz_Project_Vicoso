# Gene Expression Distance of F1s GE to SINICA and KAZA GE 

For each transcript, we calculate the distance in expression levels between the F1 individual and the 2 parental populations (males and females separately).

The pipeline **for F1s ONLY**. F2 is quite different because transcript ancestry must be added to the pipeline

**The bubble plot:**
Since we are looking at F1 individuals, we would expect the point to line-up on the 1:1 line on the bubble plot. Males do so, but females seems weirdly biased towards kaza. This could have potentially be expected if for example, all F1 females had inherited their W from kaza, but this is not the case: some females have sinica W. 

**What to try next?**
- normalise F1s only (exclude F2s) 
- normalise males and females separately
- look at genes that are differentially expressed between SIN and KAZ
- get rid of sex-linked transcripts
